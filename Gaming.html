Playerunknown Background

Playerunknown Background is also popularly known as PUBG. It is a player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in battle royale.The one who remain alive till last wins the game. Player can also choose if they want to start the match solo, duo or in squad where the squad will be of four people. The battle ground starts with every player will be in the plane and they can choose their destination by themselves and they will be get down with the help of parachute. If they want to play safe they will land on the places where there won't be more enemy amd if they want to fight and have a rush game than they will land where most of the enemy are there. We will find bot as well in the game. Player have to search in houses and ware house for the gun and other needed equipments. When they kill their enemy the killed person can be looted we can take their equipment if anything is needed. While playing duo or squad you can also talk with your teammates and communicate while battling in the ground. Player need to move from one place to another because the maps begins to shrink down towards a random location, if the player remain outside the play zone area they need to take the damage or the players may also die if they have low health. After every game the players earn certain game currency based on their performance. The game currency will be used to buy crates which contain equipments or new dresses for the players.
